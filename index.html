Kamikazee

Genres:
Alternative Rock, Pop Punk, Hard Rock, Hardcore Punk, OPM

Year Formed:
2000

Labels:
Universal Records Philippines Inc.

Members:
Jay Contreras, Jomal Linao, Led Tuyay, Puto Astete, Bords Burdeos

Facts About Kamikazee:
Kamikazee are iconic Filipino rockers whose music is a mix of Pinoy pop, punk, rock, and metal.
Although their music is vulgar, incorporating many references to genitalia, a lot of Filipinos still admire their music.
This band is known for their songs "Narda," "Chiksilog," and "Doo Bidoo."

Albums:
Kamikazee (2002), Maharot (2006), Long Time Noisy (2009), Romantico (2012)

Eraserheads

Genres:
Alternative Rock, Pop, Experimental, Original Pinoy Music (OPM)

Year Formed:
1989

Labels:
BMG, Musiko, Sony BMG Music Entertainment

Members:
Ely Buendia, Buddy Zabala, Marcus Adoro, Raimund Marasigan

Facts About Eraserheads:
Eraserheads is one of the most successful Filipino rock bands.
This band is sometimes dubbed "The Beatles of the Philippines."
They are considered one of the most influential and significant bands in the Philippine music industry.
They are known for their songs "Ang Huling el Bimbo," "With a Smile," and "Spoliarium."

Albums:
Ultraelectromagneticpop (1993), Circus (1994), Cutterpillow (1995), Fruitcake (1996), Sticker Happy (1997), Natin99 (1999), Carbon Stereoxide (2001)

December Avenue

Genres:
Pop Punk, Pop Rock, Alternative Rock, Emo Music, Indie Pop, Original Pinoy Music (OPM)

Year Formed:
2007

Labels:
Tower of Doom Records

Members:
Zel Bautista, Jem Manuel, Don Gregorio, Jet Danao, Gelo Cruz

Facts About December Avenue:
December Avenue is an indie pop and alternative rock band in the Philippines.
This Pinoy rock band is one of the most-streamed bands in the Philippine music industry.
They were nominated for multiple awards at the Wish 107.5 FM Music Awards in 2019.

Albums:
December Avenue (2016)
